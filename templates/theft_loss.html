Reporting Guidelines

Timeline

Report to DEA within 1 business day of discovery of theft or significant loss.

DEA Form 106

Submit online at DEA Diversion Control or call 1-877-792-2873.

Law Enforcement

Contact local police and file a report. Retain the police report number for DEA records.

Documentation

Maintain detailed records of the incident, investigation, and all reports filed for at least 2 years.
